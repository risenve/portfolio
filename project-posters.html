Overview

Creative posters designed for a charity exhibition in Moscow — a series of graphic works exploring visual storytelling through typography, composition, and restraint. Produced in December 2023 as a personal and artistic statement.

The posters participate in annual exhibitions, have been printed in contemporary design publications, and were displayed at the Oktyabr Cinema in Moscow — one of Russia's most iconic cultural venues.

Approach

Each poster in the series was conceived as a standalone visual object — a single idea reduced to its most essential form. The work draws from editorial design, protest graphics, and contemporary art to create pieces that are both aesthetically driven and contextually loaded.

The series balances bold formal decisions with quiet conceptual depth, designed to hold attention in both print and gallery contexts.

Pairs & Variations

Several posters in the series were produced in paired variations — exploring how a single concept shifts in meaning when rendered through different visual systems, colour relationships, or typographic structures.
